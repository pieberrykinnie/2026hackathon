# NimbleRate

*Zero-touch, hyper-local dynamic pricing that moves as fast as the market.*

In the hotel business, demands move fast, and so rates should adjust. If you sell a bed too cheap during a massive surge, you've left money on the table. Dynamic pricing solves this by matching the exact right price to real-time demands.

Right now, giants like Marriott use million-dollar algorithms to change their prices by the minute to capture that value. But according to Skift Research, a staggering 90% of independent hotels still rely on static, manual pricing spreadsheets.

Because they fail to react to real-time demand, small business owners are losing up to 20% of their revenue every single year. Think about New York. When the NYC Marathon rolls around, or a massive concert hits Madison Square Garden, legacy software catches it for the big chains. The 30-room boutique hotel in Brooklyn? They miss out entirely.

That’s why we built NimbleRate. We are giving that 90% an affordable, zero-touch pricing engine that uses real-time local data to put their revenue on autopilot, so they stop leaving money on the table.
